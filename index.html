About Royal Spice

For over two decades, Royal Spice has been serving the finest Indian and Mughlai cuisine in Varanasi. Our master chefs blend traditional recipes with contemporary techniques to create unforgettable dining experiences.

We take pride in using only the freshest ingredients and authentic spices sourced directly from local markets. Every dish is prepared with love and attention to detail, ensuring that each bite transports you to the royal courts of India.
